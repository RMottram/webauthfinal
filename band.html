Jeremy Wade McKinnon was born on the 17th December 1985 in Gainesville, Florida. Jeremy is the founding person of A Day To Remember and is also their lead vocalist. Jeremy's interest in music first came when he use to hang out with one of his friends and his band. Jeremy's first band was called 'All for Nothing' before he joined up with guitarist Tom Denney and drummer Bobby Scruggs to form A Day To Remember.

Joshua Woodward was born on the 29th May 1986 in Orlando, Florida. Josh is one of the original members of the band since its inception back in Ocala, Florida in 2003. Josh is the lead bassist for the band and was known for helping the band rise to the top via one of his friends who helped get the band into contact with an employe at Victory Records who later signed them on in 2006 before the release of their second album 'For Those Who have Heart'.

Kevin Skaff was born on the 8th June 1985 in Owatonna, Minnesota. Originally, Kevin was not one of the original members of the band, Kevin joined A Day To Remember in 2009 when he had to replace Tom Denney mid-tour due to a broken wrist. Since then Kevin has been a valued member of the band helping release three albums. Kevin is the bands lead guitarist as well as being Jeremy's backup vocals with fellow band member Neil Westfall.

Alex Shelnutt was born on the 15th August 1990 in Ocala, Florida like fellow band mate Neil Westfall. Alex joined the band back in 2006 when he was only 15 years old which resulted him to ask his mother to see if it was ok with her for him to join the band. He replaced the drummer at the time Bobby Scruggs who was the original drummer since the band started in 2003.

Neil Westfall was born on the 25th December 1987 in Ocala, Florida. Neil is the third member of the group that has been part of the band since its inception aback in 2003. Since then he has been one of the guitarist mainly in having the role of rhythm guitarist and at the same time in assisting fellow band member Kevin Skaff in being part of the backing vocals for lead vocalist Jeremy McKinnon.

ADTR were originally formed back in 2003 from Ocala, Florida. Lead vocalist Jeremy McKinnon, guitarists Neil Westfall and Tom Denney, bassist Josh Woodard and drummer Bobby Scruggs financed their first EP themselves then later on signing with Indianola Records for their first full-length record in 2005 'And Their Name Was Treason'. One year later the band signed with Victory Records to put together their second full-length record, 'For Those Who Have Heart' which was released in 2007 along with the newest member Alex Shelnutt replacing Bobby Scruggs on drums. In 2009, their third full-length record 'Homesick' was released towards the end of 2009. Tom Denney left the band after the third record was released which allowed Kevin Skaff to join. The fourth record 'What Separates Me From You' was released just over a year after in 2010. In 2011 a dispute occured involving the band and Victory Records that lead to the lawsuit of the Victory Records and allowed ADTR to produce and self-release their next album 'Common Courtesy' which was released in 2013. The album hit No 37 on the Billboard 200 and highly charted in several other nations. The band has now got their sixth full-length record 'Bad Vibrations' and has several top rated singles such as 'Paranoia' and 'Bad Vibrations'. The album reached No 2 in USA, No 6 in the UK and No 1 in Australia.
